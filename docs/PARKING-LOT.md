# Parking lot (deferred — not pilot scope)

Items we explicitly defer until after TPW/NB pilot sign-off.

## Contact roles (member, staff, volunteer, guest, director, vendor)

**Today:** Every person is a **Contact** per tenant. Operators use:

- `interestType` / `source` — how they arrived (newsletter, event, import, etc.)
- `type` — pilot-light: Lead/inquiry or Event registrant only
- `tags` — freeform labels (`staff`, `vendor`, …) when needed ad hoc

**Later:** First-class contact roles with clear UI, filters, and permissions — not the same as formal **Membership** (tiers, Stripe, renewals on the Memberships tab).

Do not expand role enums or CRM vocabulary until NB/TPW operators finish clean-slate testing.
