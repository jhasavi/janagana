## Your Beta Organization Setup

### 1. Access Your Dashboard

Your organization has been created with the following details:
- **Organization Name**: [Provided during setup]
- **Admin Email**: [Provided during setup]
- **Access URL**: `https://[your-slug].orgflow.app/dashboard`

### 2. Complete the 5-Minute Setup Wizard

When you first log in, you'll be guided through a quick setup wizard:
1. Upload your organization's logo
2. Set your primary brand color
3. Configure your timezone
4. Set up your support email
5. Invite your first team member (optional)

### 3. Import Your First Members

OrgFlow makes it easy to import your existing member database:

**Option A: CSV Import**
1. Download our CSV template from the Members section
2. Fill in your member data
3. Upload the CSV file
4. Map columns and complete the import

**Option B: Manual Entry**
1. Go to Members → Add Member
2. Fill in the member details
3. Save and repeat for each member

**Option C: Sign-up Link**
1. Go to Members → Settings
2. Copy your public sign-up link
3. Share it with your members to let them self-register

### 4. Create Your First Event

1. Navigate to Events → Create Event
2. Fill in event details:
   - Event name and description
   - Date, time, and location
   - Ticket types (if charging for attendance)
   - Event image
3. Set registration settings
4. Publish the event
5. Share the event page with your members

### 5. Invite Your Team

Add admins and staff members to help manage your organization:
1. Go to Settings → Team
2. Click "Invite Team Member"
3. Enter their email and select their role
4. They'll receive an invitation to join
